Revolutionizing Irish Potato Farming with Artificial Intelligence

Our Mission

Our Vision

A future where every farmer has instant access to crop disease diagnosis and expert agricultural guidance at their fingertips.

Critical Window for Treatment

Irish potato farmers in Uganda lose up to 50% of their harvest to Late Blight and Early Blight diseases. Traditional diagnosis requires expert visits that can take days, by which time the disease has spread. MONDIcare provides instant, accurate diagnosis using just a smartphone photo.

🦠 Diseases We Detect

⚠️ Late Blight

Phytophthora infestans - The most destructive potato disease. Causes dark lesions and white fungal growth.

High Severity

⚠️ Early Blight

Alternaria solani - Causes dark concentric rings on leaves. Common in warm, humid conditions.

Medium Severity

✅ Healthy Plant

Vigorous growth with no disease symptoms. Maintain good farming practices.

No Treatment Needed
